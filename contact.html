Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions.

What subjects do you cover?
I tutor Finance, Accounting, Economics, and Business Statistics at the high school, undergraduate, and graduate levels — including CFA and CPA preparation. If you are unsure whether I can help with your specific course, send me a message with your syllabus and I will let you know.
Do you offer in-person sessions?
Yes. I offer both online sessions via Zoom and in-person sessions in the Halifax area. Most students prefer online for convenience, but in-person is available if that works better for you.
How much does tutoring cost?
Individual sessions are $40 CAD per hour, pair sessions are $35 per student, and group sessions (3 or more) are $30 per student. No contracts or packages required. See the full breakdown on the pricing page.
Is the first consultation really free?
Yes. The initial 15-minute consultation is free with no strings attached. It gives us a chance to talk about your goals, see if we are a good fit, and plan your sessions.
Can you help with my assignments?
Yes — we work through your assignments together step by step so you understand the material and can complete them with confidence. I will not do the work for you outright, but I will make sure you understand every part of it. That is how you actually learn.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free reschedule up to 4 hours before your session. I ask for at least 24 hours notice when booking new sessions. Life happens — just communicate and we will work it out.
How do I pay?
Payment is by e-Transfer after each session. No upfront costs, no recurring charges.
Can you help me prepare for a specific exam?
Yes. Send me your course outline, past exams, or practice problems and I will build a focused review plan for you. Exam prep is one of the most common reasons students reach out.
