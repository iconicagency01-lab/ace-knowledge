Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions.

What subjects do you cover?
I tutor Finance, Accounting, Economics, and Business Statistics at the high school, undergraduate, and graduate levels. I work with your specific course material.
Do you offer in-person sessions?
Yes. I offer both online sessions via Zoom and in-person sessions. We can discuss what works best for you during the free consultation.
How much does tutoring cost?
Individual sessions are $40 CAD per hour, pair sessions are $35 per student, and group sessions (3+) are $30 per student. There are no contracts or package requirements.
Is the first consultation really free?
Yes. The first consultation is a short conversation where you tell me about your course, where you are struggling, and what you need. There is no charge and no obligation.
How do I pay?
Payment is by e-Transfer after each session. No upfront costs, no recurring charges.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
You can reschedule for free up to 4 hours before your session. I ask for at least 24 hours notice when booking a new session.
Can you help with exams and assignments?
I help you understand the material so you can perform well on exams and complete assignments with confidence. I teach — I do not do the work for you.

Still have questions?

The easiest way to find out if I can help is to reach out. No commitment required.
