Every session is built around your actual course material — your textbook, your syllabus, your assignments. I cover four core areas from high school through graduate level.

From introductory personal finance to advanced corporate valuation. I connect the formulas to real-world decisions so you understand the reasoning, not just the math.

High SchoolUndergraduateGraduate

Topics covered

Time value of money, NPV, IRR, capital budgeting, CAPM, portfolio theory, bond pricing, stock valuation, financial statement analysis, working capital, mergers and acquisitions, risk management.

02

I make the debits and credits make sense. Whether you are preparing financial statements for the first time or working through cost allocation at the graduate level, I build your understanding from the ground up.

High SchoolUndergraduateGraduate

Topics covered

Journal entries, adjusting entries, financial statements, accounts receivable, inventory, depreciation, bonds payable, cost accounting, managerial accounting, budgeting, variance analysis, consolidations.

High SchoolUndergraduateGraduate

Step by step, no intimidation. I help you build confidence with numbers so you can interpret data, run analyses, and actually understand what your results mean.

High SchoolUndergraduateGraduate
